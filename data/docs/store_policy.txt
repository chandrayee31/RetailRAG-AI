Retail Store Policy Document

Refund Policy:
Customers can return products within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Shipping Policy:
Orders are processed within 2 business days. Standard shipping takes 5–7 days. Express shipping takes 2–3 days.

Discount Policy:
Seasonal discounts are applied during major holidays. Loyalty members receive an additional 10% discount.

Customer Support:
Customer support is available via email and chat from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.

Return Exceptions:
Electronics and perishable items are non-refundable unless defective.

Warranty Policy:
Electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Warranty claims require proof of purchase.
